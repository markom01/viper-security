# ADR 005: Fleet Image Sourcing

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The fleet section needs an image of a Mercedes S-Class. No proprietary vehicle photos are available yet.

## Decision
Use free stock photography from Unsplash or Pexels (Mercedes S-Class or equivalent luxury sedan) as placeholder images. These will be replaced with real vehicle photos when available.

## Consequences
- Lower loading time risk (stock images are CDN-hosted)
- Visual placeholder until real photos arrive
- Must ensure license compliance (Unsplash/Pexels are free for commercial use)
- Task 8 updated to use Unsplash image URLs
